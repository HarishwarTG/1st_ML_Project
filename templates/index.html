Medical Insurance Price Prediction

Welcome to the Medical Insurance Price Prediction Project

This project aims to develop a machine learning model that can predict medical insurance charges based on various factors such as age, sex, BMI, number of children, smoking status, and region.

Project Overview

The project involves collecting and preprocessing data, exploring and visualizing the data, building and training machine learning models, and deploying the best model for prediction. The goal is to provide an accurate and reliable tool for individuals to estimate their medical insurance charges and make informed decisions.
