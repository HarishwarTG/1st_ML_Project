Welcome to the Medical Insurance price prediction Project

This project aims to develop a machine learning model that can predict a student's performance in mathematics based on various factors such as gender, ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, test preparation course, and scores in reading and writing.

Project Overview

The project involves collecting and preprocessing data, exploring and visualizing the data, building and training machine learning models, and deploying the best model for prediction. The goal is to provide an accurate and reliable tool for educators and students to assess their potential performance and take necessary steps to improve their academic outcomes.
